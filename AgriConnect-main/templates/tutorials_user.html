How AgriConnect Works — For Users

{% if g.user and g.user.role == 'user' %} ← Back to Dashboard {% else %} ← Back Home {% endif %}

1. Register & Browse

Create a "User" account from the homepage. Once logged in, you can click "Marketplace" to browse all available products from our verified farmers.

2. Filter & Find

Use the "Show Filters" button to find exactly what you need. You can search by product name, category, or the farmer's location.

3. Order & Pay

Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. You can fill in your delivery address and choose to pay with Cash on Delivery (COD) or securely online with UPI, cards, and more.

4. Track & Rate

In your "My Dashboard," you can see your order history and track the status (e.g., Processing, Shipped). After you receive your order, you can leave a rating for the farmer from the "Rate a Farmer" tab.
