FAQs & Platform Guidelines

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a product?

Simply browse our marketplace, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout! You can pay with Cash on Delivery (COD) or securely online with UPI.

How do I become a farmer?

Click "Register" on the homepage and choose "Register as a Farmer." You will need to submit proof of identity (like an Aadhaar card) and proof of your agricultural activity (like land papers) for review by our admin team.

Platform Guidelines

For Customers

We expect all users to be respectful in their communications. If you have an issue with a product, please file a complaint or a return request. Do not use abusive language in ratings or messages.

For Farmers

You must sell only high-quality, genuine products as described in your listings. Any farmer found to be selling fake or low-quality goods, or engaging in spam, will have their account suspended.
