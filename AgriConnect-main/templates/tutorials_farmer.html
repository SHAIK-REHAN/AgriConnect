How AgriConnect Works — For Farmers

{% if g.user and g.user.role == 'farmer' %} ← Back to Dashboard {% else %} ← Back Home {% endif %}

1. Register for Verification

Create a "Farmer" account from the homepage. You must submit your contact details, a Government ID, and proof of agriculture (like land papers) so our admins can verify you.

2. Get Approved

You will receive an email once our admin team has approved your application. Once approved, you can log in to your Farmer Dashboard.

3. Add Your Products

In your dashboard, you can add new products, including name, description, category, price, quantity, and photo. You can also use the AI Tools to get smart price recommendations based on your location and live market rates!

4. Manage Your Sales

When a customer places an order, you will receive an email. You can see all your new orders in the "My Orders" tab. From here, you can update the order status from "Processing" to "Shipped" and "Delivered" to keep the customer updated.
